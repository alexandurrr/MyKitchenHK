About Us

Learn More About Us

At My Kitchen, we're passionate about serving authentic Tibetan cuisine that's halal certified, fresh, and cooked to order. Our menu features a variety of dishes that are inspired by traditional Tibetan recipes, and we take pride in using only the highest quality ingredients to ensure that every dish is full of flavor and nutrition.

  • Traditional Tibetan Food
  • Halal Certified
  • Cooked to order Momo Tibetan Dumplings

One of our specialties is our momo Tibetan dumplings. These delicate and savory dumplings are filled with a variety of meats and vegetables, and they're a favorite among our customers. We make our momos fresh to order, so you can enjoy them hot and steaming from the kitchen. We believe that food is more than just sustenance - it's an experience that brings people together. That's why we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere at My Kitchen, where you can enjoy great food, connect with others, and relax in a comfortable setting. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a leisurely dinner, we hope you'll join us at My Kitchen for an unforgettable meal.
Thank you for choosing My Kitchen, and we look forward to serving you soon.
